About Me

I am an engineer, passionate about teaching, innovation, and the design process. My work blends technical rigor with creative problem‑solving, focusing on empowering students to think like engineers through hands‑on projects, modern tools, and real‑world applications.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe engineering education should be:

  • Practical — grounded in authentic engineering challenges
  • Visual — using diagrams, simulations, and clear communication
  • Scalable — supported by automation and reproducible workflows
  • Collaborative — encouraging teamwork and iterative design
  • Innovative — integrating modern tools and engineering design thinking
