Introduction to Engineering Design

Introduction to Engineering Design is a project‑driven course that teaches students how engineers approach problems, develop ideas, and build solutions. Through hands‑on activities, teamwork, and iterative design, students learn the foundations of engineering practice and creative problem‑solving.

The course emphasizes communication, prototyping, and the engineering design process — preparing students for advanced engineering coursework and real‑world challenges.

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply the Engineering Design Process to real problems
  • Generate and evaluate design concepts
  • Create sketches, models, and prototypes
  • Test, refine, and iterate on designs
  • Communicate engineering ideas clearly and visually
  • Collaborate effectively in engineering teams
